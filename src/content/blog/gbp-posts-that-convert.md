GBP posts are not a magic ranking button—but they are a trust and recency signal, and they give you room to showcase seasonal offers homeowners actually need in Wisconsin.

**Cadence that works**

- **Weekly offer or reminder** — Tune-ups before the first heat wave, sump checks before spring rains, gutter clears before fall.
- **Proof post** — Before/after or “here is what we found” (within customer comfort and privacy).
- **Education** — Short, plain-language explainers on high-ask topics: heat pumps, panel upgrades, IAQ.

**What to avoid**

- Keyword-stuffed posts that read like 2011 SEO.
- Stock photos that never show your trucks, your techs, or your market.
- Promising response times your dispatch cannot keep.

**AI assist, human approval**

Draft batches of posts a month ahead, then adjust for weather and inventory (equipment lead times matter). The goal is consistency without burning out your office manager every Friday afternoon.

Measure what matters: calls, direction requests, and booked jobs—not vanity “views” alone.
